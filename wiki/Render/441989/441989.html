Rawr.Retribution

Rawr.Retribution is a tool to help Retribution Paladins choose and optimize their gear, from brand new 85s, to endgame raiders.

Current Status

This model is currently Partially-functional, maintained by Caromina, and you should not rely on it for your gear choices in Cataclysm yet. 

Fully working:

  • Damage numbers for all skills
  • Buffs / Talents in general (Exceptions see below)

Partitially working:

  • AoE Rotation (Targets are averaged out for now)

Non working / Known Issues:

  • Mana usage
  • Avenging Wrath
  • Heroism / Bloodlust

Options Pane

Seal => What seal will be used.

Inquisition·refreshed·below·X·sec => Below X seconds it will refresh inquisition as soon as you have enough holy power.

HP·used·for·inquisition => Holy power used to refresh inquisition.

Wait·for·Crusader·Strike·if·CD·< => If the CD of CS is < X sec, it will wait and cast CS instead of casting a filler.

Display Statsgraph => DPS change chart depending on - 100 ... 0 ... +100 of the most important stats.
