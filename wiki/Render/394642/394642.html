Rawr.ProtWarr

Rawr.ProtWarr is a tool to help Protection Warriors choose and optimize their gear, from brand new 85s, to endgame raiders.

Current Status

This model is currently fully functional with minor omissions, consistently maintained by EvanM, and you can safely rely on it for your gear choices in Cataclysm. There may be a few talents or abilities that are not 100% modeled. If you find something that is incorrect please follow the Posting Guidelines and create an Issue in the Issue Tracker.

Recent Updates

  • Fully updated for 4.0.6 patch
  • Added user-selectable Shield Block uptime and Shield Block modeling
  • Added modeling of Heavy Repercussions in conjunction with Shield Block uptime

Planned for Next Update

  • More accuracte automatic modeling of Vengeance rather than manual selection
  • Rage modeling to automatically calculate maximum Heroic Strike uptime and Inner Rage usage
  • Rend usage in single-target rotations

Planned for Future Updates

  • AoE threat mode
  • Full BossHandler integration

Not Planned or Undefined

  • Shield Wall or Last Stand averaging (not planned)
  • Self-healing abilities (undefined value)
