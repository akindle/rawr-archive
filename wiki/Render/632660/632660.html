Rawr.Enhance

Rawr.Enhance is a tool to help Enhancement Shamans choose and optimize their gear, from brand new 85s, to endgame raiders.  It incorporates an "Export to EnhSim" (http://enhsim.codeplex.com) feature which allows users who want a deeper analysis of their stats to copy n' paste their stats directly into EnhSimGUI.  For the vast majority of users however Rawr.Enhance aims to be more than adequate for their needs.

Current Status

This model is currently non functional, maintained by Timetodance, and you may not rely on it for your gear choices in Mists of Pandaria.
