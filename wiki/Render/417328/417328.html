Rawr.Cat

Rawr.Cat is a tool to help Cats choose and optimize their gear, from brand new 85s, to endgame raiders.

Current Status

This model is currently fully functional, consistently maintained by Astrylian, the head of the Rawr project, and you can safely rely on it for your gear choices in Cataclysm.

Options Pane

Custom Rotation

  • Rip: Enforces the usage of Rip in the rotation
  • Shred: Enforces the usage of Shred in the rotation
  • Rake: Enforces the usage of Rake in the rotation
  • Savage Roar CP: Savage Roar will only be performed at this or above CP
  • Ferocious Bite CP: Ferocious Bite will only be performed at this or above CP
  • Mangle for T11 4P: When you have the T11 4P set bonus, this will enforce that you are Mangling to keep the Bonus active
  • Lag Variance (ms): The in-game latency you normally play under. This can be viewed in game on the primary menu bar where you see character, questlog, talents, help etc buttons.

Misc

  • Trinket Offset: This indicates that you unequip and then reequip one of your trinkets before you begin attacking in order to desynchronize their proc timers. This is benefitial when you have two trinkets whose procs provide such stats which have caps (such as Armor Penetration) so that you can gain the benefit of both a majority of the time.
