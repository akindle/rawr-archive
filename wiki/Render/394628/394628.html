Rawr.HealPriest

Rawr.HealPriest is a tool to help Holy and Discipline Priests choose and optimize their gear, from brand new 85s, to endgame raiders.

Current Status

This model is currently non-functional, consistently maintained by TNSe and Scottix, and you should not rely on it for your gear choices in Cataclysm.

Explanation of Comparison Values

  • HPS - Burst : This is simply your HPS without any restrictions on your mana. Unlimited mana? This is what you get
  • HPS - Sustained: This is your HPS adjusted for fight length and based on your mana regeneration. If you go OOM just as fight ends, this is your estimated HPS.
