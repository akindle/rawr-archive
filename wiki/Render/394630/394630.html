Rawr.ShadowPriest

Rawr.ShadowPriest is a tool to help Shadow Priests choose and optimize their gear, from brand new 85s, to endgame raiders.

Current Status

This model is currently partially functional, consistently maintained by Shep1987, TNSe and DiscoMurray, and you should not rely on it for your gear choices in Cataclysm.
