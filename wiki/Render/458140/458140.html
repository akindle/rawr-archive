Rawr.RestoSham

Rawr.RestoSham is a tool to help Restoration Shamans choose and optimize their gear, from brand new 85s, to endgame raiders.

Current Status

This model is currently mostly-functional, being rewritten and consistently maintained by Antivyris, and you should not rely on it on it's own for your gear choices in Cataclysm.
